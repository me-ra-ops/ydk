YAGNA DHRUVA 2026 is a flagship techno-cultural fest that brings together innovation, creativity, and competition. The event serves as a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, artistic talents, and collaborative spirit.

With a diverse range of events, workshops, and competitions, YAGNA DHRUVA aims to inspire learning beyond classrooms while fostering teamwork, leadership, and excellence.
